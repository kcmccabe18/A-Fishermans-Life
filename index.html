Fish the Gulf like a local, at affordable prices!

Located in Clearwater, we're just a 30 minute drive from Downtown Tampa and 15 minutes from Downtown St. Petersburg. All Captains are USCG licensed and boats are USCG inspected and serviced regularly. We will handle everything for you, including tools, and safety equipment, so that you can have the best time possible! All you have to do is show up in comfortable attire and enjoy your time on the water.

Whatever your fishing level is, we will out staff will help to make sure that you have the time of your life!

  • Mahi Mahi
  • Grouper
  • Tuna
  • Swordfish
  • Snapper
  • Tarpon
